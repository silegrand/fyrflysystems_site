The Problem It Solves

Security Without Infrastructure

No mains power available

Remote construction sites, rural highways schemes, border zones and defence installations often have no grid connection within reach. The Fyrfly Solar CCTV Tower generates and stores its own power from a 1,000W solar array and 440Ah battery bank — fully operational day and night, in any season.

No fixed infrastructure

Traditional CCTV requires ground works, cable runs and permanent mounting structures. The Fyrfly Solar CCTV Tower requires none of this. It arrives on a trailer, deploys on adjustable stabilising legs across uneven ground, and can be repositioned as site requirements evolve — without a single trench being dug.

No time for conventional installation

Emergency deployments, temporary events, border incidents and rapid-response security operations cannot wait weeks for an installation programme. The Fyrfly Solar CCTV Tower is operational in under 90 minutes from arrival on site, with full remote access and live monitoring from the moment it powers up.

Platform Capabilities

Every Capability. One Platform.

The Fyrfly Solar CCTV Tower integrates six distinct technology systems into a single deployable unit — surveillance, detection, deterrence, communications, power and intelligence.

Power System

Solar-Powered, Battery-Backed

A 1,000W monocrystalline solar array charges a 440Ah LiFePO4 battery bank, delivering continuous 24V DC power. Onboard diagnostics monitor state of charge in real time. Optional backup power capability ensures operation during extended low-light periods in northern latitudes or dense cloud cover.

Surveillance

Multi-Camera Configuration

The standard configuration supports a motorised PTZ camera with optical zoom, fixed wide-angle HD cameras for full perimeter coverage, and a thermal imaging camera for detection through complete darkness, fog and foliage. All channels record to onboard encrypted storage with a minimum 33-day retention capacity and encrypted cloud synchronisation.

AI Analytics

Intelligent Detection Engine

Edge-processed AI analytics run directly on the tower without dependence on a cloud connection. Capabilities include virtual tripwire and zone crossing, loitering detection with configurable dwell thresholds, vehicle and person classification, PPE compliance detection, fire and smoke detection, and perimeter alert escalation to the monitoring centre.

Communications

4G/5G and Site-Wide Wi-Fi

Dual-SIM 4G/5G connectivity provides the primary link to the monitoring centre, cloud storage and remote management portal. An integrated Wi-Fi access point with site-wide coverage capability means the tower can simultaneously serve as the communications hub for a temporary site — connecting site offices, welfare units and other security devices from day one.

Audio Deterrence

Integrated PA System

A high-output PA horn delivers both live operator audio challenges and pre-recorded automated deterrence messages triggered by AI detection events. When combined with LED deterrent lighting, this creates an active response capability that goes beyond passive recording — audibly challenging intruders in real time, 24 hours a day.

Built to Endure

Engineered for Extreme Conditions

The powder-coated, corrosion-resistant steel cabinet is IP66-rated against water and dust ingress, with dual-sealed doors, concealed hinges and tamper-alert sensors. The 4.5m anti-wind mast deploys to a stable 2m servicing height and is engineered to Eurocode wind load standards. Operating temperature range: -22°C to +55°C.

Common Questions

What You Need to Know

The 1,000W solar array charges the 440Ah LiFePO4 battery bank during daylight hours. The battery provides power through the night and during periods of reduced solar yield. In standard UK conditions the system is designed for up to five days of autonomous operation without solar input — covering extended periods of heavy overcast. For northern deployments in winter or long-duration operations with consistently low solar yield, an external generator input port is available as a backup charging source.
In standard conditions, a two-person Fyrfly team can have the tower fully operational — mast extended, cameras live, monitoring centre connected and remote access confirmed — in under 90 minutes from arrival on site. The unit does not require any ground anchoring, cable runs or civil works. The adjustable stabilising legs level the platform across uneven ground, and the forklift and pump truck slots allow precise positioning without manual lifting.
Yes. The camera system is compatible with all major Video Management System platforms via standard ONVIF and RTSP protocols. If you have an existing VMS or security operations centre, the tower's camera feeds can be integrated directly. Our technical team will confirm compatibility during the pre-deployment consultation and configure the integration before the tower goes live on site.
Detection events trigger a configurable escalation protocol. The system can automatically activate the PA deterrent with a pre-recorded audio challenge, alert the Fyrfly NSI-approved Alarm Receiving Centre for visual verification, push a notification to your site management team, and begin high-frame-rate recording of the event. The response protocol is defined with you before deployment so that every alert follows the right escalation path for your site and operating hours.
All footage is recorded to encrypted onboard solid-state storage with a minimum 33-day retention capacity at full resolution. Live and recorded footage is accessible remotely via an encrypted web portal and mobile application. Where connectivity permits, footage is simultaneously synchronised to encrypted cloud storage for off-site redundancy. UK GDPR compliance documentation is provided as standard, including a CCTV policy template and retention schedule for the duration of the deployment.
The tower is designed for low-maintenance operation. Onboard diagnostics continuously monitor battery health, solar yield, camera function, network connectivity and tamper status — reporting to the Fyrfly management portal in real time. This enables proactive servicing before any issue affects site performance. For deployments exceeding three months, a scheduled service visit is recommended to clean the solar panels, inspect cable connections and update system firmware. Fyrfly provides a managed service option covering all maintenance for the duration of the deployment.
