The Challenge

CCTV in Education and Public Sector Is Never Straightforward

Schools must balance safeguarding pupils and staff with the rights and dignity of children. Public buildings must monitor openly accessible spaces while meeting strict data protection and Surveillance Camera Code obligations. Generic CCTV systems designed for retail or commercial use simply don't fit these environments.

Fyrfly Systems designs every CCTV installation around your specific environment, your compliance obligations, and your duty of care. We don't just install cameras — we design systems that capture what matters, protect the people in your care, and stand up to legal scrutiny.

Safeguarding ObligationsSchools must follow DfE guidance and Keeping Children Safe in Education — CCTV placement and policies must be defensible to Ofsted and insurers.
Complex Building LayoutsMulti-storey schools, large campuses, listed buildings and NHS estates require careful camera placement and infrastructure planning to achieve full coverage.
Data Protection and RetentionUK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018 and the Surveillance Camera Code impose specific requirements on how footage is stored, accessed and deleted.
Out-of-Hours CoverageSchools and public buildings face different security risks in the evening and at weekends — perimeter monitoring and remote access are essential.
Our CCTV Services

HD & 4K Camera Systems
Core Service · Most Specified

High-resolution camera systems that produce evidential-quality images in all lighting conditions. We specify the right camera technology for each location — from wide-angle coverage of open areas to varifocal lenses for entrance points and perimeters — ensuring every camera earns its place.

NVR & Cloud Recording
Storage & Management

Network Video Recorder (NVR) systems and cloud storage solutions configured to meet your data retention obligations. We set retention schedules, access controls and audit trails aligned to your data protection policy and the Surveillance Camera Code.

  • On-site NVR with redundant storage for resilience
  • Hybrid cloud options for multi-site access
  • Configurable retention periods aligned to GDPR requirements
  • Role-based access control for footage review
Video Analytics
Intelligent Monitoring

Intelligent analytics that reduce false alarms and surface genuine security events. Motion detection, line crossing, loitering detection and people counting help schools and public buildings get more from their CCTV investment without increasing staffing costs.

  • Line crossing and zone intrusion detection

  • People counting for occupancy management
  • Integration with monitoring centre for automated response

Surveillance Camera Code of Practice
Public sector CCTV operators have specific obligations under the Home Office Surveillance Camera Code. We design systems that meet all 12 guiding principles from the outset — not as an afterthought.
UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018
We document lawful bases for processing, configure retention periods, establish Subject Access Request procedures and provide the documentation your DPO needs to demonstrate compliance.
DfE & Keeping Children Safe in Education
School CCTV must be addressed in the school's safeguarding policy. We advise on camera placement, signage, policy wording and governor reporting to ensure full compliance with DfE requirements.
BS EN 62676 — CCTV for Security Applications
All installations are designed and commissioned to BS EN 62676, the British and European standard for CCTV systems, ensuring evidential quality and insurance compliance.
