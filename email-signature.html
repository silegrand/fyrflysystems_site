How to add this to Gmail

  1. Select everything in the grey box below (Ctrl+A inside it, or click and drag)
  2. Copy it (Ctrl+C)
  3. Open Gmail → Settings (gear icon) → See all settings
  4. Click the Signatures tab → Create new
  5. Paste into the signature box (Ctrl+V) — formatting will be preserved
  6. Edit your name, job title, phone and email directly in the box
  7. Click Save Changes
