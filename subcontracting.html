Where We Add Most Value

The Projects Other Subcontractors Struggle With

Our background in sensor systems, public sector compliance and technically complex deployments means we're comfortable in the scenarios that trip up generalist installers. These are the jobs where the right subcontractor makes the difference.

Martyn's Law Compliance Projects

Schools and public venues needing to close the gap between their current physical security provision and the requirements of the Terrorism (Protection of Premises) Act 2025. We understand the legislation, design to it, and produce the documentation that satisfies the Responsible Person's compliance obligation.

Multi-Building Campus Projects

School campuses, hospital estates, university sites and local authority building portfolios: multi-building projects where a single coherent security infrastructure needs to span physically separate structures. We design for the whole estate, not just each building in isolation.

Listed Buildings & Heritage Sites

Security installations in listed buildings, Victorian schools and heritage properties where cable routing is restricted and structural intervention must be minimised. Our wireless infrastructure expertise means we can achieve comprehensive CCTV and access control coverage without the cable routes that would compromise a listed structure.

New Build to Programme

Construction projects where M&E security packages need to be designed, installed and handed over within the main contractor's programme. We work to building project timelines, coordinate with other trades, and produce the O&M documentation the client's facilities team needs on practical completion.

System Upgrades & Legacy Migrations

Existing analogue CCTV or outdated access control systems that need upgrading to IP-based infrastructure, often while the building remains in full operation. We manage the transition, minimise disruption to the client's operations, and ensure the new system is fully commissioned before the old one is decommissioned.

Out-of-Region Coverage

You've won a contract in an area where you don't have engineers. Rather than turning the work down or taking a risk on an unknown local installer, use Fyrfly as your delivery partner. We cover projects across England and Wales, with documentation and sign-off processes that integrate cleanly into your own project management framework.

How We Work

Clean, Simple, Professional

We've designed our subcontracting process to be as frictionless as possible for the lead contractor. Four steps from first contact to completed handover, with full documentation at every stage.

01
Initial Briefing

You share the project scope, programme and any existing site documentation. We sign an NDA at this stage if required. No obligation, no cost.

02

03

04
Handover Pack
