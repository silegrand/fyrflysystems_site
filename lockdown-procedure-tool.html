Step 3 — Signals & Communications

How will lockdown be signalled?

The DfE template requires you to define distinct signals for lockdown initiation and stand-down. These must be different from your fire alarm signal.

Must be clearly distinguishable from the fire alarm. Consider a verbal announcement over PA as a backup.

The stand-down must be given verbally by a named, recognised person to prevent confusion.

Select all that are available at your school. The DfE recommends silent communications wherever possible.
