About These Estimates
All figures are indicative budgetary estimates based on typical installations of similar size and complexity. Actual costs will vary depending on your specific site, building layout, cable runs, existing infrastructure and specification. These estimates include supply, installation and commissioning. They do not include ongoing maintenance contracts, monitoring fees or VAT. A free site survey is required for an accurate, costed proposal.
Why We Show Both Options
Some companies will quote Entry Level CCTV at a low price and tell you the specification is identical to Enterprise — which is often technically true for resolution and image quality. What they do not tell you is that outdoor hardware degrades differently, replacement cycles differ significantly, and AI analytics capability is not comparable. We believe informed customers make better long-term decisions, and we would rather earn your trust than win a price comparison.
