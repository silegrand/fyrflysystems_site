The Challenge

Open Buildings, Controlled Access — Getting the Balance Right

Schools are required to be welcoming and accessible, yet Keeping Children Safe in Education demands that every person on site is known, vetted and purposeful. NHS buildings serve the public, yet must restrict access to clinical areas, data rooms and staff-only zones. Local authority offices handle sensitive personal data, yet must remain accessible to the residents they serve.

The answer isn't locked doors everywhere — it's intelligent, role-based access control that opens the right doors for the right people at the right times, and creates a complete record of every event.

Safeguarding on School SitesSchools must prevent unauthorised adults from accessing areas where children are present — without creating a fortress environment that undermines a positive learning culture.
NHS and Healthcare EnvironmentsPatient safety areas, controlled drug stores, data rooms and staff-only zones require tiered access — accessible to the right clinicians and staff, not to visitors or the public.
Contractor and Visitor ManagementSchools and public buildings host contractors daily. Managing who is on site, checking credentials and maintaining records is a safeguarding and insurance obligation that manual sign-in books simply can't satisfy.
Out-of-Hours LettingsSchools and leisure facilities let spaces to community groups outside hours. Time-restricted access allows lettings without handing out master keys or requiring staff to be on site.
Our Access Control Services

Access Control Built Around Your People and Your Site

From single-door entry systems to estate-wide multi-site platforms, we design and install access control solutions that fit your environment and your compliance obligations.

Door Access & Reader Systems
Core Service · Most Specified

Card, fob, PIN and biometric reader systems that control access to individual doors, zones and buildings. We specify the right credential technology for each environment — proximity cards for staff, PIN-only for low-security areas, biometric for high-security zones — and integrate everything into a single management platform.

  • Proximity card, fob and smart card readers
  • PIN and combination credential options
  • Biometric fingerprint and facial recognition for high-security areas
  • Electric strikes, magnetic locks and electromechanical locks
  • Fail-safe and fail-secure configurations for fire safety compliance
Visitor Management
Safeguarding Essential

Digital visitor management systems that replace paper sign-in books with a compliant, auditable process. Pre-registration, photo ID capture, DBS status flagging and printed visitor badges — all integrated with your access control system to grant time-limited site access automatically.

  • Pre-registration and pre-screening for expected visitors
  • Photo capture and printed visitor badges on arrival
  • DBS status alerts and watchlist checking
  • Evacuation reports showing all visitors on site
Video Door Entry & Intercoms
Reception & Perimeter

IP video door entry systems that let reception staff see, speak to and grant access to visitors from their desk — without leaving the office. Integrated with your access control and CCTV for a complete picture of who is at each entrance point.

  • HD video door stations at main entrances and gates
  • Desk-based and mobile app answer points
  • Remote door release from any location
  • Call logging and video evidence retention
Multi-Site Management
MAT & Estate Capability

Centralised access control management for Multi-Academy Trusts, local authorities and NHS estates with multiple buildings. Manage credentials, zones and access levels across your entire estate from a single platform — add a new staff member once, grant access everywhere they need it.

  • Single platform for all sites across your estate
  • Centralised credential management and provisioning
  • Estate-wide reporting and audit trails
  • HR system integration for automated joiner/leaver processes
Reporting & Audit Trails
Compliance & Governance

Comprehensive access event reporting for compliance, insurance and safeguarding purposes. Who unlocked which door, when, using which credential — all timestamped and exportable. Automated muster reports for fire evacuations show exactly who was in the building at any given moment.

  • Full timestamped access event log
  • Automated fire muster and roll call reports
  • Scheduled and on-demand compliance reports
  • Exportable data for insurance and audit purposes
Keeping Children Safe in Education (KCSiE)
Schools are required to control site access and maintain records of all visitors. Our visitor management systems satisfy KCSiE obligations with a fully auditable, digital process that paper sign-in books cannot replicate.
UK GDPR — Data Minimisation and Retention
Access control data is personal data. We configure retention periods, access logs and data deletion schedules aligned to your data protection policy and GDPR accountability obligations.
Fire Safety — Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order
All access control hardware is specified to fail-safe in fire conditions — doors release automatically on fire alarm activation. Muster reports from the access control system support evacuation procedures.
PAS 24:2022 — Enhanced Security Performance
Electrically operated door hardware is specified to PAS 24 where required by insurers or local authority security policies, providing an enhanced level of physical security for sensitive door sets.
