Capabilities

What AI Analytics Can Do for Your Site

These are not experimental features. They are proven, deployed capabilities available in the systems we install today.

Virtual Tripwires
Draw a line anywhere on the camera view. Any person or vehicle crossing it in either direction triggers an immediate alert. Ideal for perimeter boundaries, restricted area entrances and out-of-hours monitoring.
Real-time alert
Loitering Detection
Define a zone and a time threshold. If a person remains in that zone longer than the defined period, an alert is sent. Particularly effective for car parks, building entrances and areas with a history of anti-social behaviour.
Configurable threshold
Forensic Video Search
Search hours of recorded footage in seconds. Specify clothing colour, vehicle type, direction of travel or time window. The system returns timestamped clips from across all cameras — what used to take hours now takes seconds.
Seconds not hours
People Counting
Monitor occupancy in real time. Automatic counting of people entering and leaving defined areas — useful for capacity management, evacuation accountability and understanding how your site is actually used.
Real-time occupancy
Vehicle Detection & ANPR

Automatic plate reading

Compliance

Built for UK Schools and Public Sector Law

AI analytics raises specific compliance questions that do not apply to traditional CCTV. Facial recognition, biometric data processing and automated decision-making all carry higher obligations under UK GDPR. We are explicit about what our systems do and do not do.

Every AI analytics package we supply is configured and documented to comply with UK GDPR, the Surveillance Camera Code of Practice and KCSiE. We do not install capabilities that we cannot help you evidence compliance for.

Permitted
Object and behaviour detection Detecting objects, behaviours and events without identifying specific individuals. Tripwires, loitering detection and crowd analysis do not process biometric data and sit within standard GDPR legitimate interests.
Permitted
Attribute-based forensic search Searching footage by clothing colour, approximate size or vehicle type does not constitute biometric processing and is permissible under existing CCTV lawful basis with appropriate documentation.
Permitted
Vehicle detection and ANPR Number plate reading for access management is permissible with a documented lawful basis, clear signage and a retention policy. We provide the documentation templates.
Requires advice
Facial recognition Facial recognition processes biometric data — special category data under UK GDPR. The threshold for lawful deployment in schools and public spaces is very high. We advise on this case by case and will not install it where compliance cannot be evidenced.
